Our Story

Four years in.
Every build counts.

BoostHouse MotorWorks was founded in 2021 with one mission: give Subaru owners a performance engine builder they could actually trust.

Four years later, we're still doing it the same way. Hand-assembled. Measured twice. Built to outlast whatever comes next.

Hands On
Our Philosophy

The motor is only as good as the hands that built it.

Anyone can order parts. What separates a motor that lasts from one that grenades is the attention to tolerance, the patience during assembly, and the willingness to stop and re-measure when something doesn't feel right.

We don't cut corners because we can't live with the outcome. Every block we ship carries our name. That's not a slogan, that's the standard.

What We Stand For

Three things we won't compromise on.

01

In The Shop
