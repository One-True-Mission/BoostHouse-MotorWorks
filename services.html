Our Process

Clear steps. No surprises.

Every build follows the same four phases. You'll know what's happening, when, and why at every point.

Consultation

We talk horsepower goals, budget, and timeline. You get a clear spec sheet and an honest quote before anything gets ordered.

Teardown & Spec

Motor comes apart on our benches. Every tolerance measured, every part accounted for, every decision documented.

Assembly

Hand-built to tolerance. Balanced rotating assembly, blueprinted clearances, torque-plate honing where it matters.

Verification

Leak-down tested before it leaves. You get photos, a build sheet, and a motor ready to drop in and run.
