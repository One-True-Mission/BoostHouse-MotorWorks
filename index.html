About The Shop

Purpose-built power, assembled by hand.

BoostHouse MotorWorks was founded on a simple standard: every motor leaving our shop should outperform, outlast, and out-engineer whatever the customer drove in with. We specialize in Subaru EJ25 and FA20DIT platforms, with packages ranging from OEM-spec rebuilds to closed-deck, forged-internal competition blocks.

No shortcuts. No upsells. Just a team of experienced builders who care about the motor as much as you do.
